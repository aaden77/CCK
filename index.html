About CCK

The Compassion Corporation of Kingston (CCK) was established many years ago with the mission to take an active interest in the civic, cultural, social, and moral welfare of the community. We strive to nurture compassion, drive positive change, and empower our citizens to create a thriving, inclusive and vibrant community.

Our Activities

At CCK, we are passionate about making a difference. Our programs include community outreach, cultural events, civic engagement workshops, and more.

Community Outreach

Programs dedicated to supporting underprivileged populations while enhancing community solidarity.

Cultural Events

Organizing events to celebrate and preserve our unique cultural heritage through art, music, and traditions.

Civic Engagement

Hosting forums and workshops to encourage active and ethical participation in civic matters.
